Admission Requirements

Join the next generation of kingdom leaders

Academic Requirements

  • WASSCE/SSCE certificate or equivalent qualification
  • Minimum of 6 credits including English Language
  • Transcript from previous educational institutions
  • Personal statement of faith and calling

Spiritual Requirements

  • Evidence of personal relationship with Jesus Christ
  • Active membership in a local church
  • Pastoral recommendation letter
  • Clear sense of calling to ministry
