About OBI

Oasis Bible Institute is a premier theological education institution dedicated to training and equipping men and women for effective ministry in the 21st century.

Through rigorous biblical education, practical ministry experience, and spiritual formation, we prepare students to serve as pastors, teachers, missionaries, and Christian leaders in various fields.

Our programs combine academic excellence with spiritual depth, ensuring graduates are both knowledgeable and spiritually mature.
