About Us

Home About Us

Our Mission

To be an oasis of hope and transformation, bringing the life-giving message of Jesus Christ to all people. We are committed to empowering communities through spiritual guidance, biblical education, and practical support that transforms lives and glorifies God.

Our Vision

To be a leading Christian ministry in Ghana and West Africa, known for excellence in biblical teaching, transformative discipleship, and impactful community development that reflects the love and power of Jesus Christ.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Biblical Authority

We believe the Bible is the inspired Word of God and the foundation for all our teachings and practices.

Love & Compassion

We demonstrate Christ's love through compassionate service to all people, regardless of background.

Community

We build strong, supportive Christian communities where people grow together in faith.

Excellence

We pursue excellence in all aspects of ministry, giving our best for God's glory.

Service

We serve others with humility and dedication, following Jesus' example of servant leadership.

Integrity

We maintain the highest standards of honesty, accountability, and ethical conduct.

Our Story

Oasis IMG Ministries was founded over 15 years ago with a simple yet powerful vision: to be a place of spiritual refreshment and transformation for all who seek God.

From humble beginnings in a small community gathering, God has expanded our ministry to touch thousands of lives across Ghana. Today, we operate multiple ministries, including our renowned Oasis Bible Institute, youth programs, women's fellowship, and community outreach initiatives.

Our journey has been marked by God's faithfulness, as He has used ordinary people to accomplish extraordinary things for His kingdom. We continue to trust Him for greater impact in the years ahead.

What We Believe

Our foundational beliefs

The Trinity

We believe in one God, eternally existent in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

Salvation

We believe salvation is found in Jesus Christ alone, through faith and by God's grace.

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God and our ultimate authority.

The Church

We believe in the universal Church as the body of Christ, called to worship, fellowship, and service.

The Great Commission

We believe in fulfilling Christ's command to make disciples of all nations.

Eternal Life

We believe in the resurrection of the dead and eternal life for all who trust in Christ.
